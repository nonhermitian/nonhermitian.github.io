Kaleidoscope is a visualization package for quantum computation that focuses on interactivity. In addition, there is a range of functionality for the Qiskit quantum computing framework for visualizing quantum systems and their associated properties.

Kaleidoscope is released under the Apache 2 license. Kaleidoscope is copyright IBM as I work there, however it is not an official or unofficial IBM product.
